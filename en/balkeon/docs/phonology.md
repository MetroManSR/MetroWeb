## Phonotactics  

### Core Restrictions  
1. **No triphthongs** ‚Üí Use `-r-` separator  
   - *Bad:* "miaa"  
   - *Good:* "miara"  

2. **Forbidden initials** ‚Üí Add `e-` prefix  
   - *Bad:* "spokeon"  
   - *Good:* "espokeon"  

3. **Clustered consonants** ‚Üí Simplify  
   - *Bad:* "kh", "bp"  
   - *Good:* "k", "p"  

### Loanword Adaptation
